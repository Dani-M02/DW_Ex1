Who am I

Hi! My name is Daniela, I'm 21 and I'm from the north of Portugal, the shoe's capital, Felgueiras. I could go study design for that area, but I decided to choose another type of design. Got my degree in Design e Multimédia in Faculdade de Ciências e Tecnologia of Universidade de Coimbra between 2020 and 2023, and at the moment I'm doing the masters of the same course. While I depeen my knowlegde in Design, Media e Programming, I'm also trying to start my professional career in Visual Arts, more precisely Illustration and Animation, sharing my work on social media and doing commissions per request. My interests are considered more on the "nerdy" side. I'm a fan of anime, mangá/webtoons and videogames, being perticulary interested in fantasy themes. Music is also one of my fonts of inspiration, being open to anything but my top of choice are Indie, Rock e Phonk.
